BayPiggies Meeting, December 2013

Date Wed 01 January 2014 Tags Python / BayPiggies

I attended and filmed the speaker at the December BayPiggies meeting at LinkedIn. The speaker was Peter Farrell, a mathmatics teacher at a company called learningtech.org.

All in all it was a very interesting talk, showcasing a cool use-case of Python in an educational setting. For another really interesting watch, check out Peter's YouTube video about modeling eliptical orbits with visual python
